# Privacy Policy for DriveNest

**Last Updated:** March 20, 2026

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to **DriveNest**. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy informs you about how we handle your data when you use our application.

## 2. Data Collection
DriveNest is a client-side application. We do not operate central servers that collect your personal files.
- **Google Account Data:** During Google authentication, we receive an encrypted token to access your Google Drive. This token is stored locally on your device.
- **File Data:** The application reads your Google Drive files to synchronize them with your local folder. This data is **never** transmitted to any third party other than Google.

## 3. Usage
We use your Google Drive access solely to:
- Sync files between your local machine and your Google Drive account.
- Provide a file management interface for your personal use.

## 4. Security
We use system-level security (Keytar/Keychain) to store your credentials. No data is stored in the cloud by our application.
